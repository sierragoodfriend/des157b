Overview

For my capstone project, I want to explore what the future would look like if we implemented rent controlled communal living spaces/complexs rather than having individual housing units.

Audience

My audience is pretty expansive! I want to reach people who cannot afford a house and want alternatives to consider. I also want to reach people who have experieneced the loneliness that can sometimes accompny living in small family units.

Housing has become a means of profit rather than a basic need. I want to propose and alternative to the direction we are going in now.

Focal Points

In DES 157b, we've done a lot of preparation work for our proposal by looking through various futuritic lenses. I plan on using speculative design in order to demonstrate the current state of our housing system, and then to show/imagine possible futures with more inclusive & communal housing.

I will also be looking at dystopian and utopian ideas of the future – our world without adequate housing for the majority perhaps? Or a world with more communal living, child assistance, social interaction, affordable housing...

Features of My Project

I hope to have interactive features like a poll – asking people if they would consider buying a property with a friend for example and having a two family unit. Then asking bigger questions – like would you live in a communal living complex.

I believe that a scroll function could be useful in keeping users entertained while they digest information at a rate they are comfortable with.

Concerns

I don't think communal living would be implemented smoothly. For example, I feel like if you try to keep a space clean with others, there can be people that fall short and others who have to pick up their slack.

I also have concerns with safety issues of living in larger communities – but I believe that more supportive / small scale community environments could potentially help eliminate safety issues over time.
